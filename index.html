🧮 How We Calculate This

Simple math to reveal the brutal truth about your GTM efficiency:

Annual GTM Burn = (BD Team + Marketing Team) × Average Salary
Cost per Active Partnership = Annual GTM Burn ÷ Live Partnerships
Activation Rate = (Live Partnerships ÷ Announced Partnerships) × 100
Annual Waste = Annual GTM Burn × (1 - Activation Rate)

This assumes your GTM team's primary focus is partnerships. Real waste is probably higher when including events, tools, and travel.
